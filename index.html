Our Story

WHAT: Founded in 2020, Clinity is a nonprofit organization based in Fremont, CA. We host volunteer events, where volunteers can earn valuable experience in a medical environment. Members can receive service hours for their work, which can be used to qualify for the Presidential Service Award.

WHO: Our founders are a group of seven high school students, all passionate about community service.

WHOM: Anyone who signs up 1 week prior to volunteering, are able to commute to their nearest hospital.

WHY: We strive to assist underprivileged communities with ease of medical facilities. More than 27 million Americans do not have access health care. Through Clinity, we hope we can inspire volunteers to help our communities, while developing a passion for medicine.
